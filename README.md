# 🐦 Flappy Bird: Aviation Disaster Simulator

Welcome to **Flappy Bird**, a game where a bird with questionable flying skills attempts to navigate through an endless series of pipes designed by a certified psychopath.

## 🎮 About The Game

You are a bird.

The pipes are your enemies.

Gravity is also your enemy.

Actually, everything is your enemy.

Your mission is simple:

* Don't hit the pipes.
* Don't hit the ground.
* Don't lose your sanity.

Good luck.

---

## 🚀 Features

✅ Bird goes up when you click.

✅ Bird goes down when you don't click.

✅ Pipes appear out of nowhere.

✅ Score increases when you somehow survive.

✅ Emotional damage included for free.

---

## 🎯 How To Play

1. Start the game.
2. Click/Press Space to flap.
3. Miss a pipe.
4. Question your life choices.
5. Restart.
6. Repeat steps 2-5 forever.

---

## 🏆 Winning Condition

There is no winning.

You just survive longer than your previous attempt and pretend that's progress.
